Overview

The Yamaha YZF R6 is a sport motorcycle introduced in 1999. It has a 599 cc liquid-cooled 4-stroke engine that produces 117 horsepower and 45 lb-ft of torque. The R6 has a top speed of around 160 mph and can go from 0 to 60 mph in under 3 seconds.

The R6 is known for its sharp handling and agility, making it a popular choice for track riding and racing. It has a sleek and aerodynamic design with a twin-spar aluminum frame and a distinctive front fairing.

The current price of Yamaha R6 in Nepal is 39.99 lakh.

Specifications

  • Horsepower: 117 hp
  • Torque: 45 lb-ft
  • Top speed: 160 mph
